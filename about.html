Who We Are

A Dream Born in the Heart of Kathmandu

Hotel White Dreams was established in 2025 with a simple but powerful vision: to create a hotel where every guest feels genuinely welcomed, comfortably accommodated, and warmly looked after. Nestled in Koteshwor-32, one of Kathmandu's most well-connected neighbourhoods, we set out to build something that Kathmandu could be proud of.

Our hotel features 28 thoughtfully designed rooms, ranging from affordable Standard Non-AC rooms to premium Deluxe AC rooms, all crafted to offer maximum comfort with modern amenities. From the moment you arrive at our entrance to the moment you check out, our team is committed to making every detail of your stay exceptional.

Located just 2.3 kilometres from Tribhuvan International Airport, we've become the preferred choice for transit travellers, business guests, families visiting Kathmandu, and local events. The name "White Dreams" reflects our aspiration, pure, bright, and filled with possibility, just like every great journey begins.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Genuine Hospitality

We treat every guest as a welcomed friend. Our team is trained to anticipate your needs, respond with warmth, and go the extra mile to make your stay memorable.

Safety & Security

Your safety is our highest priority. Our hotel is equipped with 24-hour CCTV surveillance and trained security guards so you can relax with complete peace of mind.

Uncompromising Cleanliness

We maintain the highest standards of hygiene and cleanliness across all rooms, common areas, and the restaurant. A fresh, clean environment is non-negotiable for us.

Value for Money

We believe comfort shouldn't cost a fortune. Our pricing is transparent and fair, offering premium amenities at rates that respect your budget.

Prime Location

Strategically located in Koteshwor just 2.3km from TIA Airport, we offer maximum convenience for all types of travellers, business, leisure, or transit.

Modern Comfort

Built with modern design principles, our hotel blends contemporary aesthetics with functional comfort, from high-speed WiFi to AC rooms and a fully equipped restaurant.

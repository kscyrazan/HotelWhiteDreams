Our Restaurant

Flavours That Bring You Home

At Hotel White Dreams, we believe that great food is an essential part of a great stay. Our in-house restaurant is a warm, welcoming space where skilled chefs bring you the best of Nepali home cooking alongside popular Indian and Continental dishes, all made fresh, every day.

Whether you're starting your morning with a hearty breakfast before heading to the airport, refuelling at lunch after sightseeing in Kathmandu, or winding down with a relaxed dinner, our kitchen is ready to serve you.

What We Serve & When

🌅

Breakfast

6:30 AM - 10:00 AM

Start your day right with freshly cooked eggs, toast, Nepali chiya (tea), fresh fruit, dal bhat, and more. Our breakfast is hearty enough to fuel a full day of Kathmandu exploration.

☀️

Lunch

11:30 AM - 3:00 PM

Dinner

6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Wind down with a relaxed dinner featuring all our signature dishes, special evening sets, and drinks at the bar. Perfect for groups, couples, and families.

Hotel Policies

Policies & Information

Please review our hotel policies before your stay. Our team is happy to answer any questions via WhatsApp.

Check-in & Check-out

Check-in: 1:00 PM
Check-out: 12:00 Noon (Noon)
Early check-in and late check-out are available on request, subject to availability. Please contact us via WhatsApp in advance to arrange.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be notified in advance. Please contact us as early as possible if your plans change. We will do our best to accommodate rescheduling. Direct bookings via WhatsApp are flexible, reach out to discuss your situation.

ID & Guest Policy

A valid government-issued ID (Citizenship Card, Passport, or Driving License) is required at check-in for all guests. ID must be provided for each adult in the group. Children are welcome when accompanied by adults.

Smoking Policy

Hotel White Dreams is a non-smoking property. Smoking is strictly prohibited in all rooms, corridors, and dining areas. Designated outdoor areas may be used for smoking.

Payment Methods

We accept:
• Cash (NPR) - Direct payment at reception
• Card Payment - Debit and credit cards accepted
• QR Payment - eSewa, Khalti, IME Pay and all major digital wallets
Full payment is required at or before check-in. Advance deposits may be required for group bookings.

Pet Policy

Pets are considered on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us in advance via WhatsApp to discuss your requirements. We appreciate your cooperation in ensuring a pleasant stay for all guests.

Security & Safety

Hotel White Dreams operates 24-hour CCTV surveillance throughout the property and employs trained security guards. We take the safety of all our guests very seriously. Please report any concerns to reception immediately.

Child Policy

Children of all ages are warmly welcome at Hotel White Dreams. Children staying in the same room with parents using existing bedding are accommodated at no extra charge. Additional beds can be arranged for a small fee. Please inform us at the time of booking.

About Us

Where Every Stay Feels Like Home

Nestled in the heart of Koteshwor, Kathmandu, Hotel White Dreams offers a seamless blend of modern comfort and warm Nepali hospitality. Opened in 2025, our hotel was designed to give every guest, whether traveller, business visitor, or family, a stay that truly feels like a dream.

Located just 2.3 km from Tribhuvan International Airport, we're the ideal choice for travellers seeking convenience without compromising on quality. With 28 thoughtfully furnished rooms, a full-service restaurant, and modern conference facilities, we have everything you need for a perfect stay in Nepal's capital.
